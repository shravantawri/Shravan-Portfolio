April, 2021

loan repayment status

The main objective of this study is to use personal information along with data provided by credit card applicants to predict the likelihood of future defaults and credit card borrowings. Thus, banks can use the findings of this project as an important factor in deciding whether to issue a credit card to the applicant or not. The data available to solve this task includes features such as client number/ID of the applicant along with their gender, number of children, annual income, education level, marital status, age, employment status, occupation, family size etc. Another dataset contains the loan repayment status for various applicant IDs over the past several months. Applicants have been categorised into numerous groups which are explained in the methodology section of the report.
